What is MUIDS Press?

Since the dawn of mankind, the media has played an essential role in various factors such as politics, entertainment and most importantly education.

Our objectives are to provide people with free access to educational resources, as well as, to allow students to partake in journalism. Students are encouraged to promote their research works on the website while also maintaining their own credibility of the work.
